Timed Essay

The timed essay was a two-part assignment in which I first needed to analyze some graphs and data and then write an essay supported by the data and my takeaways. The writing portion of this assignment was done with a time constraint. In terms of 21st Century Skills, I believe that this assignment best targeted information skills. Being a good writer indeed helped with this assignment, but the most vital part was analyzing and interpreting all of the different data presented before the essay. I am proud of how I did with this assignment because it reflects much of what I learned last year. I took both English and Statistics courses at the college last year, and this assignment combined elements I learned from both of those courses.

Botique Hotel

For this assignment, I was tasked to build the floor plan for a two-story boutique hotel. There were a multitude of requirements for this assignment, including the specific rooms, their placement, and size. Completing this assignment, I had to think carefully and critically, as changing even the slightest detail could have implications for the entire project. I am most proud of the improvisation I had to do to fit all the guest rooms in my design. There was a moment where I felt I might have to restart the assignment, but I had to think outside the box to make my design work.

Socratic Seminar

For the Socratic seminar assignment, I first had to generate what I thought were thought-provoking questions and discussion topics relating to early civilization or not relating to it at all. Then, I was put in a group of students who had all done the same, and we discussed our questions in a Socratic seminar. In the past, I have done social seminars, but they were never very organized and felt forced. During this assignment, I learned what a proper socratic seminar should look like, and I believe I was able to demonstrate growth by only interrupting someone else once.
